Historia

Welcome! We hope you enjoy viewing our 48-hour XD-project, Historia.


Additional Details

Historia was a 48-hour project created for the February 2020 Adobe + A&E Networks Creative Jam Live, solely using Adobe XD. Historia won first place in this competition, beating 55+ teams across North America. This app creates a simple research solution for high-school and university students, where it essentially acts as a smart-tool to curate research sources.
